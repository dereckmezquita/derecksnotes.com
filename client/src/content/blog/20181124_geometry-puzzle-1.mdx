Here is a fun problem. It is a geometrical problem, and the goal is to prove that the area of A is equal to the area of B.

![Drawing of geometric problem](/blog/20181124_geometry-puzzle-1/20181124_geometry-circles-area.svg)

The parameters of the problem are simple; the largest external curve is a quarter circle, the two inner curves are half circles, they are all considered perfect circles.
