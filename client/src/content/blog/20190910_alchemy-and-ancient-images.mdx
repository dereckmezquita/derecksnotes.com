Alchemy, a fascinating subject, once seen as a science. Now we know it was all but; it was a pseudoscience, yet can we believe that the pursuit of such a subject was done in earnest? With great minds such as Issac Newton digging into the matter, even investing considerably more energy into the subject than the very science which made him famous; was he on to something?
