<a id="pathway">Pathway</a> - A series of interactions among [molecules](#molecule) in a [cell](#cell) that result in a certain product or change in the [cell](#cell). An example is a metabolic pathway where a linked-set of chemical reactions, [catalysed](#catalysation) by enzymes, result in a product or set of products.
