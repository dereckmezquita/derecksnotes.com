<a id="rtpcr">rtPCR</a> - Reverse transcription polymerase chain reaction (see [PCR](#polymerase_chain_reaction_pcr)), this technique uses a special form of [polymerase](#polymerase) to reverse the [transcription](#transcription) process. This allows for us to go from a sample of [RNA](#rna) to the associated [DNA](#rna).
