<a id="basal">Basal</a> - Baseline level. For example, there might be basal [gene expression](#geneExpression) in the absence of activators or in the presence of repressors. This is the minimum amount of [gene expression](#geneExpression). The basal expression level can drastically affect the performance of systems, *exempli gratia* in positive autoregulation.
