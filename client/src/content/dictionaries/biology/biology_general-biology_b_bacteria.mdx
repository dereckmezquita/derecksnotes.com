<a id="bacteria">Bacteria</a> - Bacteria are a [microorganisms](#microOrganism), classified as [prokaryotes](#prokaryote). They most often present themselves in a [unicellular](#unicellular) form and always lack a [nucleus](#nucleus). Their [genetic material](#gene) floats freely inside the cell and can be organised into two different sets, a [central circular chromosome](#circular_prokaryote_chromosome) and [plasmids](#plasmid).
