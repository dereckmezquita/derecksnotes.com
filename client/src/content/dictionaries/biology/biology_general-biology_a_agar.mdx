<a id="agar">Agar</a> - Agar, not to be confused with [agarose](#agarose) is a jelly like substance extracted from red algae known as [agarophytes](#agarophytes) in the [Rhodophyta](#rhodophyta) [phylum](#phylum). Agar is a mixture composed of about 30% [agaropectin](#agaropectin) and 70% [agarose](#agarose) (used in [electrophoretic gels](#electrophoresis)). Agar is used as a solid medium for microbiology.
