<a id="scanning_electron_microscope_sem">Scanning Electron Microscope (SEM)</a> - A special kind of microscope that scans samples with a high-energy beam of electrons to produce a high-resolution, detailed, three-dimensional image. An SEM can magnify a sample up to 250 times that of the best light microscopes.
