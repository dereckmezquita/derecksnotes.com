<a id="association_constant_binding_constant">Association Constant (Binding constant)</a> - A special case of the equilibrium constant *K*. It is the inverse of the [dissociation constant](#dissociation_constant). It describes the binding and unbinding of a receptor (R) and a ligand (L).

$$
    R + L \leftrightarrows RL
$$

$$
    K_a = \frac{k_{on}}{k_{off}} = \frac{[RL]}{[R][L]}
$$

 See also [dissociation constant](#dissociation_constant).
