<a id="anticodon">Anticodon</a> - A sequence of [nucleotides](#nucleotide) that are complementary to [codons](#codon). These are typically found in [tRNA](#trna) which allows for the matching of an [amino acid](#amino_acid) to a [codon](#codon).
