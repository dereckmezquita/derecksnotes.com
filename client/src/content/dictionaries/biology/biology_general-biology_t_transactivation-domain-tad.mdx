<a id="transactivation_domain_tad">Transactivation Domain, TAD</a> - A domain of a [transcription factor](#transcription_factor_tf) that interacts with the [transcriptional](#transcription) machinery, cofactors, and/or [chromatin](#chromatin) modifying machinery (eukaryotes only) in order to facilitate formation of the [transcriptional initiation complex (TIC)](#tight_junction).
