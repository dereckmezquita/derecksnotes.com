<a id="crispr_cas9">CRISPR Cas9</a> - A new tool based on the bacterial CRISPR-Cas system to target and cut [DNA](#dna_desoxyribonucleic_acid), thus allowing for targeted [genome](#genome) editing. CRISPR stands for clustered regularly interspaced short palindromic repeats.
