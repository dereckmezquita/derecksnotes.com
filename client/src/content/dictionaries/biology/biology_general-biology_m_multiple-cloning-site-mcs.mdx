<a id="multiple_cloning_site_mcs">Multiple Cloning Site (MCS)</a> - A designed stretch of [DNA](#dna_desoxyribonucleic_acid) with recognition sites for many common [restriction enzymes](#restriction_enzyme). This region allows for the easy design, management, and [cloning](#cloning_molecular_cloning) of [DNA](#dna_desoxyribonucleic_acid) into larger environments, such as [plasmids](#plasmid).
