<a id="codon">Codon</a> - A triplet (three) of [ribonucleotides](#ribonucleotides) in [mRNA](#mrna) that encodes for an [amino acid](#amino_acid).

 Note that whether or not a codon matches this table depends on the organism. Some organisms, especially [microorganisms](#microOrganism) and [bacteria](#bacteria) often present modified readings of these codons (known as alternative codons). This is dependant on the presence or not of a [tRNA](#trna) capable of matching the [DNA](#dna_desoxyribonucleic_acid) sequence (codon).
