<a id="flow_cytometry">Flow Cytometry</a> - A laser based technology employed for counting [cells](#cell), [cell](#cell) sorting, and [biomarker](#biomarker) detection. It works by suspending cells in a stream of fluid and passing them by an electronic detection apparatus.
