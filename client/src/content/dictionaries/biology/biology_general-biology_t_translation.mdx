<a id="translation">Translation</a> - The process of creating a [peptide](#peptide) i.e. [protein](#protein) (sequence of [amino acids](#amino_acid)). *Exempli gratia* a [protein](#protein) or [enzyme](#enzyme)) based on the sequence of an [mRNA](#mrna). The [ribosome](#ribosome) is the key [enzyme](#enzyme) in this process, which also requires [tRNA](#tRNA)s charged with [amino acids](#amino_acid). [Amino acids](#amino_acid) are linked together through [peptide bonds](#protein).
