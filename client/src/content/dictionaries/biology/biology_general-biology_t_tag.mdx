<a id="tag">Tag</a> - A tag enables an experimenter to follow or extract a particular [molecule](#molecule), molecular species or even whole cells. *Exempli gratia* the His-tag enables experimenters to enrich for species containing said tag using columns (a nickel column for the His-tag). [Fluorescent](#fluorescence) tags enable measurement with devices like plate readers or [flow cytometers](#facs).
