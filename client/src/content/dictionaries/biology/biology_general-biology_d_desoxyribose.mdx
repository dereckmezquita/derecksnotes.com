<a id="desoxyribose">Desoxyribose</a> - Desoxyribose is a form of sugar, this kind is special in that it is included in genetic material of the [DNA](#rna) kind. [RNA](#dna_desoxyribonucleic_acid) includes another kind known as [ribose](#ribose).
