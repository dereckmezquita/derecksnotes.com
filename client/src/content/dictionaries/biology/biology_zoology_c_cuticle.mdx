<a id="cuticle">Cuticle</a> - In animals, a multilayered, extracellular, external body covering, usually composed of fibrous molecules such as chitin or collagen, and sometimes strengthened by the deposition of minerals such as calcium carbonate. A waxy layer which seals the outer surface of land plants, helping to retain moisture.
