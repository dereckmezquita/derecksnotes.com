<a id="autotroph">Autotroph</a> - Any organism that is able to manufacture its own food. Most plants are autotrophs, as are many protists and bacteria. Contrast with consumer. Autotrophs may be **Photoautotrophic**, using light energy to manufacture food, or **Chemoautotrophic**, using chemical energy.
