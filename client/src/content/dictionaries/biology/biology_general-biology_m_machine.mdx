<a id="machine">Machine</a> - A set of parts that use energy to perform an action. A biological example is [transcriptional](#transcription) machinery: a set of [proteins](#protein) harness the energy of nucleotide triphosphate bonds to polymerize [RNA](#rna) transcripts.
