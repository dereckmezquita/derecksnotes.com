<a id="moclo">MoClo</a> - Modular cloning (MoClo) a hierarchical [DNA](#dna_desoxyribonucleic_acid) assembly method built on [Golden Gate](#golden_gate) technology. The use of a parts with standardised cloning sequences creates MoClo modularity. This modularity permits the automated construction of many MoClo constructs.
