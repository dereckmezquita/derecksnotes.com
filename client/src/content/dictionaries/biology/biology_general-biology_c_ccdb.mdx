<a id="ccdb">ccdB</a> - A lethal gene that targets [DNA](#dna_desoxyribonucleic_acid) gyrase. The lethal gene is useful for ensuring that the [plasmid](#plasmid) containing it cannot be propagated in standard [*E. coli*](#escherichia_coli_e_coli) strains.
