<a id="untranslated_region_utr">Untranslated Region (UTR)</a> - Sequences in the [5'](#5_prime_end_5) and [3'](#3_prime_end_3) ends of an [mRNA](#mrna) that are [transcribed](#transcription), but not [translated](#translation). UTRs often harbor regulatory elements, for example [protein](#protein) binding sites (including the [ribosome](#ribosome) binding site), [miRNA](#miRna) binding sites, [secondary structures](#conformation) *exempli gratia* [riboswitches](#riboSwitch).
