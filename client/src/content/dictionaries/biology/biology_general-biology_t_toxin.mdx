<a id="toxin">Toxin</a> - A substance (small [molecule](#molecule), peptide, [protein](#protein)) that kills cells or even whole organisms. Toxin-antitoxin pairs can be useful in biotechnology where the presence of the antitoxin confers resistance to the toxin, enabling [selection](#selection). Producing a toxin as the output of a circuit can be used in medical applications, *exempli gratia* to kill cancerous cells or infectious [bacteria](#bacteria).
