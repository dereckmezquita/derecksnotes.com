<a id="primer">Primer</a> - A short, single-stranded [DNA](#dna_desoxyribonucleic_acid) used in [DNA](#dna_desoxyribonucleic_acid) replication. A primer binds a region of single-stranded [DNA](#dna_desoxyribonucleic_acid) to form a primer-template complex which allows [DNA polymerase](#dna_polymerase) to synthesise the complementary strand (primer extension). Since most [DNA polymerases](#polymerase) are only able to catalyse the addition of a [nucleotide](#nucleotide) to the [3'](#3_prime_end_3) end of a [DNA](#dna_desoxyribonucleic_acid) strand, primers are necessary for [elongation](#elongation). Primers are a necessary reagent for [PCR](#polymerase_chain_reaction_pcr).
