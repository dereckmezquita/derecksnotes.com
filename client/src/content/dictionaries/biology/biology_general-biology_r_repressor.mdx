<a id="repressor">Repressor</a> - The suppression of [gene expression](#geneExpression) from a [promoter](#promoter) by a [protein](#protein). Important repressors in [synthetic biology](#synthetic_biology) are TetR, [LacI](#laci), and cI (lambda repressor). Some repressors are inducible.
