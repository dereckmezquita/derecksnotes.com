<a id="petri_dish">Petri Dish</a> - A petri dish is a small container used often for [cell cultures](#cell_culture). It is cylindrical and short, normally about 8.5 cm in diameter, and 1.5 cm in height. In microbiology Petri dishes are prepared with variants of [lysogeny broth](#lysogeny_broth) with [agar](#Agar) which solidifies the medium into a gell. This is used for growing [bacterial cells](#bacteria); other mediums are used for [eukaryotic](#eukaryota) or other organisms.
