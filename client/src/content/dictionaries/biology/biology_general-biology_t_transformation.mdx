<a id="transformation">Transformation</a> - The process of introducing new genetic material into cells, mostly used when talking about [bacterial](#bacteria) cells (similar to [transfection](#transfection) in [mammalian](#mammal) cells). Transformation can be performed by electrical and or chemical means, *exempli gratia* [electroporation](#electroporation).
