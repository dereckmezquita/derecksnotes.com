<a id="ligand">Ligand</a> - A [molecule](#molecule) (often small) that binds something (often much larger than the ligand) in a specific way. For example, an [inducer molecule](#inducer) that binds a [transcription factor](#transcription_factor_tf) is a ligand and caffeine is a ligand for a caffeine-specific [RNA](#rna) [aptamer](#aptamer).
