<a id="nucleotide">Nucleotide</a> - The monomeric unit of [DNA](#dna_desoxyribonucleic_acid) and [RNA](#rna). A nucleotide comprises: A [nitrogenous base](#nitrogenous_base), a five-carbon [sugar](#sugar) ([ribose](#ribose) or [desoxyribose](#desoxyribose)), at least one [phosphate group](#phosphateGroup) and an organic base. See [ribonucleotide](#ribonucleotides) for related.

 A nucleotide is made of 3 parts:

1. [Nitrogenous base](#nitrogenous_base)
2. Five-carbon [sugar](#sugar); [ribose](#ribose) or [desoxyribose](#desoxyribose)
3. One minimum [phosphate group](#phosphateGroup)

 These single units, nucleotides, are the units by which large nucleic acids DNA/RNA are constructed by polymerisation.
