<a id="screening">Screening</a> - A method to obtain a set of organisms *exempli gratia* a [bacterial colony](#bacterial_colony), with a particular [genotype](#genotype) or [phenotype](#phenotype) by considering many members of a large pool of variants. Each member is assessed for its suitability, causing more work than [selection](#selection), where the undesired members are eliminated.
