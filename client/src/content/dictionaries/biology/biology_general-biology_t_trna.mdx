<a id="trna">tRNA</a> - Transfer [RNA](#rna). These special [RNA](#rna)s have a particular structure and many chemical modifications. They are charged with [amino acids](#amino_acid); the charged tRNAs are then used in [protein](#protein) synthesis i.e. [translation](#translation). The tRNA has a three [nucleotide](#nucleotide) [anticodon](#antiCodon) loop, which complements a three [nucleotide](#nucleotide) [codon](#codon) on an [mRNA](#mrna) in the [ribosome](#ribosome), enabling the [ribosome](#ribosome) to create a new peptide chain from the sequence of an [mRNA](#mrna).
