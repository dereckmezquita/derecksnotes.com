<a id="thermostable">Thermostable</a> - Resistant to high temperature. A thermostable [enzyme](#enzyme) is still active at elevated temperatures (*exempli gratia* near boiling) and have been found in organisms that live in extreme environments, *exempli gratia* deep sea thermal vents.
