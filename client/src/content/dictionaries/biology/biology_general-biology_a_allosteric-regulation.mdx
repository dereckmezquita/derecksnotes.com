<a id="allosteric_regulation">Allosteric Regulation</a> - Allosteric regulation happens when a [conformational change](#conformation) (the allosteric part) affects the function of a [protein](#protein) or [enzyme](#enzyme). For example, a small [molecule](#molecule) may bind a [transcription factor](#transcription_factor_tf) and cause a [conformational change](#conformation) that removes the [TF](#transcription_factor_tf)'s ability to bind [DNA](#dna_desoxyribonucleic_acid), rendering it inactive.
