<a id="chassis">Chassis</a> - Cells or strain which can be used for engineering and the creation of [synthetic organisms](#synthetic_biology). It is important for these organisms to be as [minimal](#minimalOrganism) as possible or to not have any interfering metabolic pathways which those which we may want to insert.
