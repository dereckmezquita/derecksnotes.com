<a id="endonuclease">Endonuclease</a> - A nuclease that cuts **within** a given genetic (either [DNA](#dna_desoxyribonucleic_acid) or [RNA](#rna)) sequence as compared to an [exonuclease](#exonuclease) which cuts at the **extremity** of the sequence.
