<a id="origin_of_replication_ori">Origin of Replication (ORI)</a> - The location in a genome (or within a replicating [plasmid](#plasmid)) at which [DNA](#dna_desoxyribonucleic_acid) replication is initiated. [Genes](#gene) near origins of replication will typically have higher copy numbers on average.
