<a id="facs">FACS</a> - A type offlow cytometry for sorting a mixture of [cells](#cell) into two or more containers, one [cell](#cell) at a time, based upon the [fluorescent](#fluorescence) characteristics of each cell. Cells can be manipulated to express [fluorescent](#fluorescence) properties in order to sort them by FACS.
