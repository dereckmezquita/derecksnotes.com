<a id="upstream_activation_sequence_uas_puas">Upstream Activation Sequence (UAS/pUAS)</a> - A specific sequence of [DNA](#dna_desoxyribonucleic_acid) recognised by [Gal4](#gal4). The UAS [promoter](#promoter) contains multiple UAS sites and a minimal [CMV](#cytomegalovirus_cmv) core [promoter](#promoter).
