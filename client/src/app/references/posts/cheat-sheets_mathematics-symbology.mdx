Here you'll find a basic list of maths symbols which are widely used, and often times found in science. These are used throughout the site, do not hesitate to reference this sheet when needed.

## 1. Quantifiers

- $\forall$ - For all; for any; for each; for every.
- $\exists$ - There exists.
- $\exists!$ - There exists a unique.

## 2. Subset or Includers

- $ \in $ - Is an element of or belongs to. Negation: $ \notin $
- $ \subset $ - Is included in; inclusion of one ensemble in another. Negation: âŠ„
- = - Is equal to. Negation: $ \neq $
- $ \emptyset $ - An empty set.
- $ \cup $ - Union of.
- $ \cap $ - Intersection of or overlap of.
- $ \setminus $ - Deprived of, can be used to signal the difference of two sets.
- $ \vartriangle $ - Symetrical difference of.

## 3. Numbers Related to Numbers, Functions etc.

- $ \infty $ - Infinity.
- $ \ge, \le $ - Superior or equal to, inferior or equal to.
- $ \gt, \lt $ - Strictly greater than, scrictly less than.
- $ \displaystyle\sum_{k=p}^q a_k $ - The sum of $ a_k $, $ k $ a whole number variant of $ p $ at $ q $ ($ = a_p + a_p+1 +...+ a_q $)
- $ \displaystyle\prod_{k=p}^q a_k $ - Product of $ a_k $, $ k $ a whole number variant of $ p $ at $ q $ ($ = ap\times a_{p+1} \times ...\times a_q $)

## 4. Logical Connectors

- $ \land, \lor $ (AND, OR) - Conjunction, disjunction of logical propositions.
- NOT - Negation of.
- $ \implies $ - Implies or comes to.
- $ \impliedby $ - Implied by or comes from.
- $ \iff $ - Equals by deduction or equivalence.

## 5. Ensembles of Numbers

- $ \mathbb{N} $ - Set of whole natural numbers.
- $ \mathbb{Z} $ - Set of whole relative numbers.
- $ \mathbb{Q} $ - Set of rational numbers.
- $ \mathbb{R} $ - Set of real numbers.
- $ \mathbb{C} $ - Set of complex numbers.

## 6. Other symbols

- $ : $ - To denote a ratio or proportion, such as: 1:2.
