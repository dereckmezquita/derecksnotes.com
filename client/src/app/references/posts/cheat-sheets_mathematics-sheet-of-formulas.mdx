## Calculus I

$$
    \frac{df(x)}{dx} = \lim_{\Delta x \to 0} \frac {f(x + \Delta x) - f(x)}{\Delta x} = \lim_{x \to a} \frac{f(a) - f(x)}{a - x}
$$

$$
    \frac{d(f \cdot g)}{dx} = f'(x)g(x) +f(x)g'(x)
$$

$$
    \frac{d(f/g)}{dx} = \frac{f'(x)g(x) - f(x)g'(x)}{[g(x)]^2}

$$

$$

$$

$$

### L'Hôpital's rule

$$
    \lim_{x \to a} \frac{f(x)}{g(x)} = \lim_{x \to a} \frac{f'(x)}{g'(x)}
$$

## Statistics

### Gaussian distribution

$$
    f(x, \bar{x}, \sigma) = \frac{1}{\sigma \sqrt{2 \pi}} \cdot e \biggl(\frac{-(x - \bar{x})^2}{2 \sigma^2}\biggr) \, where \, \bar{x} \, is \, the \, mean.
$$

### Standard deviation

$$
    \sigma = \sqrt{\frac{1}{N} \sum_{i}^N (x - \bar{x})^2}
$$

### Variance

$$
    var(x) = \sigma^2
$$

### Binomial distribution

$$
    B(k, \, n, \, p) = \binom{n}{k} p^k (1 - p)^{n - k}
$$

### Poisson distribution

$$
    P(k, \lambda) = \frac{\lambda^k}{k!} e^{-\lambda}
$$

### Gamma function

$$
    \Gamma (t) = (t - 1)! = \int_{0}^{\infty} x^{t - 1} e^{-x} dx
$$

## Linear Algebra

### Norm

$$
    \left\lVert \vec{v} \right\rVert = \sqrt{x^2 + y^2 + z^2}
$$

### Unit vector

$$
    \vec{u} = \frac{1}{\left\lVert \vec{v} \right\rVert} \cdot \vec{v}
$$

### Dot product

$$
    \vec{u} \cdot \vec{v} = {x_1} {x_2} + {y_1} {y_2} + {z_1} {z_2} = \left\lVert \vec{u} \right\rVert \cdot \left\lVert \vec{v} \right\rVert \cdot \cos(\theta)
$$

### Inner product

$$
    \langle A | A \rangle = \begin{bmatrix} \overline{a_1} & \overline{a_2} \end{bmatrix} \left[\begin{array}{c} a_1\\a_2\end{array}\right] = \left\Vert{a_1}^2 \right\Vert + \left\Vert{a_2}^2\right\Vert
$$

### Cross product

$$
    \displaystyle \vec{u} \times \vec{v} = [u_2 v_3 - u_3 v_2, \, u_3 v_1 - u_1 v_3, \, u_1 v_2 - u_2 v_1]
$$

$$
    = \Biggl[ det \left[ \begin{array}{cc} {u_2} & {u_3}\\ {v_2} & {v_3}\end{array}\right] , - det \left[ \begin{array}{cc} {u_1} & {u_3}\\ {v_1} & {v_3}\end{array}\right] , det \left[ \begin{array}{cc} {u_1} & {u_2}\\ {v_1} & {v_2}\end{array}\right] \Biggr] = det \left[\begin{array}{ccc} \hat{i} & \hat{j} & \hat{k}\\{u_1} & {u_2} & {u_3}\\{v_1} & {v_2} & {v_3} \end{array}\right]
$$

$$
    \left\Vert \vec{u} \times \vec{v} \right\Vert = \left\Vert\vec{u} \right\Vert \cdot \left\Vert \vec{v} \right\Vert \cdot sin(\theta)
$$

### Projections

$$
    proj_{\vec{b}} \vec{a} = \biggl( \frac{\vec{a} \cdot \vec{b}}{\left\Vert \vec{b} \right\Vert ^2} \biggr) \vec {b}
$$

$$

$$

$$

$$

$$
    div(\vec{F}) = \vec{\nabla} \cdot \vec{F} = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{\delta P}{\delta x} & \frac{\delta Q}{\delta y} & \frac{\delta R}{\delta z} \end{bmatrix} \, where \, \vec{F} = [P(x, \, y, \, z), \, Q(x, \, y, \, z), R(x, \, y, \, z)]
$$

$$
    curl(\vec{F}) = \vec{\nabla} \times \vec{F} = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{\delta R}{\delta y} - \frac{\delta Q}{\delta z} \, , \, \frac{\delta P}{\delta z} - \frac{\delta R}{\delta x} \, , \, \frac{\delta Q}{\delta x} - \frac{\delta P}{\delta y} \end{bmatrix} = det \begin{pmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} \hat{i} & \hat{j} & \hat{k} \\ \frac{\delta}{\delta x} & \frac{\delta}{\delta y} & \frac{\delta}{\delta z} \\ P & Q & R \end{bmatrix} \end{pmatrix}
$$

### Laplacian

$$

$$

### Relative extremes

$$
