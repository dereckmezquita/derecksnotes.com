## 1st SEMESTER (September to **January**)

Students must attend three compulsory modules and three elective modules chosen among six, for a total of 30 ECTS.

### Summary of Modules

| Module | Title                                           | ECTS | Type         | Courses Included                                              |
|--------|-------------------------------------------------|------|--------------|---------------------------------------------------------------|
| UE0    | Refresher Courses                               | 0    | Optional     | UE101 Introduction to biology; UE102 Introduction to math & CS |
| UE1    | Foreign Language                                 | 2    | Compulsory   | UE111 English; UE112 French for non-native speakers           |
| UE2    | Synthetic Biology Practical Course               | 5    | Compulsory   | UE121 Synthetic biology practical course                      |
| UE3    | Synthetic Biology                                | 8    | Compulsory   | UE131 Genomic apps; UE132 Parts & devices; UE133 Metabolic eng.; UE134 Biosafety & ethics |
| UE4    | Cell Factory Optimisation                        | 5    | Elective     | UE141 Cell factory optimisation                               |
| UE5    | Mathematics for Systems Biology                  | 5    | Elective     | UE151 Statistical seq/gene expr.; UE152 Statistical learning networks |
| UE6    | Languages & Methods for Systems & Synthetic Bio  | 5    | Elective     | UE161 Formal methods; UE162 Test for biological objects       |
| UE7    | Nanobiology & Nanotechnology                     | 5    | Elective     | UE171 Nanobiology; UE172 Chips for molecular evolution        |
| UE8    | Rational Protein Design                          | 5    | Elective     | UE181 Computational protein design; UE182 Practical rational protein design |
| UE9    | Eucaryotic Cells Engineering                     | 5    | Elective     | UE191 Eucaryotic cells engineering                            |

### Detailed Course Tables

#### UE0: Refresher Courses (0 ECTS, Optional)

| Course | Title                                              | Description (Objectives & Syllabus)                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     | Organization                                |
|--------|----------------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------|
| UE101  | Introduction to biology                            | Overview of modern biology disciplines (molecular, genomic, systems, synthetic biology, structural biology, evolution, cell biology, bioinformatics, developmental biology). Includes practical introduction to molecular biology techniques.                               | 32h Integrated Plenary + Tutorials, 8h Practical |
| UE102  | Introduction to mathematics and computer science for biology | Basic computational and mathematical skills: probability, statistics, linear algebra, machine learning, modeling, data analysis tools (Python, R, Matlab).                                                                                                                | 40h Integrated Plenary + Tutorials          |

#### UE1: Foreign Language (2 ECTS, Compulsory)

| Course | Title                   | Description (Objectives & Syllabus)                                                                                    | Organization  |
|--------|-------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|---------------|
| UE111  | Language 1: English     | Improve language skills, scientific vocabulary, writing abstracts, PPT presentations, discussing scientific topics.       | 25h Tutorials |
| UE112  | Language 2: French (for non-native speakers) | Basics of French for everyday communication: introducing oneself, asking info, using phone/mail, buying essentials, public transport, renting flat, writing a CV. | 25h Tutorials |

#### UE2: Synthetic Biology Practical Course (5 ECTS, Compulsory)

| Course | Title                                | Description (Objectives & Syllabus)                                                                                                    | Organization     |
|--------|--------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|------------------|
| UE121  | Synthetic biology practical course   | Hands-on molecular biology (DNA cloning, bacterial transformation), from project design to data interpretation in synthetic biology.       | 40h Practical    |

#### UE3: Synthetic Biology (8 ECTS, Compulsory)

| Course | Title                                                            | Description (Objectives & Syllabus)                                                                                                                           | Organization                 |
|--------|------------------------------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|------------------------------|
| UE131  | Genomic applications in environmental biotechnology              | Sustainable bioeconomy, enzyme screening, synthetic biology methods, bioenergy applications.                                                                    | 6h Plenary + 4h Tutorials    |
| UE132  | Conception, construction & characterization of biological parts and devices | Design protein/RNA-based devices, genetic circuits in different organisms, debugging devices, directed evolution.                                         | 6h Plenary + 4h Tutorials    |
| UE133  | Metabolic Engineering                                            | Theories and techniques for metabolic network modeling, flux analysis, producing compounds in engineered E. coli strains, correlating experiments with models. | 6h Plenary + 12h Practical   |
| UE134  | Biosafety and ethical questions of synthetic biology             | Biosafety, legal frameworks, chemical toolkits as genetic firewalls, ethics, defining life, dual-use research, societal impact.                                 | 8h Plenary + 4h Tutorials    |

#### UE4: Cell Factory Optimisation (5 ECTS, Elective)

| Course | Title                    | Description (Objectives & Syllabus)                                                                                                               | Organization             |
|--------|--------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|--------------------------|
| UE141  | Cell factory optimisation| In silico optimization of bacterial cell factories, genetic manipulation, validating improved metabolite production.                               | 8h Plenary + 32h Practical |

#### UE5: Mathematics for Systems Biology (5 ECTS, Elective)

| Course  | Title                                                    | Description (Objectives & Syllabus)                                                                                                                               | Organization                 |
|---------|----------------------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|------------------------------|
| UE151   | Statistical analysis of biological sequences & gene expression (2.5 ECTS) | Markov models for sequences, HMM, profiling, PCA, multiple hypothesis testing, clustering for gene expression analysis.                                               | 12h Plenary + 8h Tutorials   |
| UE152   | Statistical learning of biological networks (2.5 ECTS)   | Machine learning for network inference, integrating heterogeneous data, dynamic models, protein-protein networks, supervised learning, active learning for experimental design. | 12h Plenary + 8h Tutorials   |

#### UE6: Languages and Methods for Systems and Synthetic Biology (5 ECTS, Elective)

| Course  | Title                                            | Description (Objectives & Syllabus)                                                       | Organization                            |
|---------|--------------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------|
| UE161   | Formal methods for biological system engineering (2.5 ECTS) | Petri nets, automata networks, process algebra, design of synthetic biology languages.     | 12h Plenary + 8h Tutorials               |
| UE162   | Test applied to biological objects (2.5 ECTS)    | Model-based testing, passive testing, complexity of structure searches, scenario databases, monitoring, validation. | 13h Plenary + 3h Tutorials + 4h Practical |

#### UE7: Nanobiology and Nanotechnology (5 ECTS, Elective)

| Course  | Title                     | Description (Objectives & Syllabus)                                                    | Organization                |
|---------|---------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|-----------------------------|
| UE171   | Nanobiology (2.5 ECTS)    | Fundamentals of nanoscience, nanopores, single-molecule analysis, nanofluidics, diagnostics, sequencing. | 12h Plenary + 8h Tutorials  |
| UE172   | Chips for molecular evolution (2.5 ECTS) | Lab-on-a-chip, microfluidics design (Autocad, PDMS chips), living cell handling on chip. | 20h Practical               |

#### UE8: Rational Protein Design (5 ECTS, Elective)

| Course  | Title                                          | Description (Objectives & Syllabus)                                               | Organization                |
|---------|------------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------|-----------------------------|
| UE181   | Computational protein design (2.5 ECTS)         | Protein modeling, docking, QSAR, knowledge-based modeling, optimization, biosensors. | 12h Plenary + 8h Tutorials |
| UE182   | Practical course on rational protein design (2.5 ECTS) | Site-directed mutagenesis, comparing wild-type/mutants, correlating with models.   | 20h Practical               |

#### UE9: Eucaryotic Cells Engineering (5 ECTS, Elective)

| Course | Title                       | Description (Objectives & Syllabus)                                                                                              | Organization  |
|--------|-----------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|---------------|
| UE191  | Eucaryotic cells engineering| Two-week internship at BIOSS (Freiburg): signaling processes, controlled release materials, nanoscale membrane analysis tools. | 40h Practical |

### Seminars

This program is enriched by seminars from international academic and industrial speakers. Students may be involved in shaping this series.
