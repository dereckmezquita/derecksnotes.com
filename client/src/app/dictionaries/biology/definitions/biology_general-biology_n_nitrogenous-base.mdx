<a id="nitrogenous-base">Nitrogenous Base</a> - A Nitrogen organic containing [molecule](#molecule), chemically is [basic](#base) and is used in the structure of genetic material [DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid) and [RNA](#rna); those used in heredity and biology are known as: **A (Adenine), G (Guanine), T (Thymine), C (Cytosine), and U (Uracil)**; used in RNA instead of T.
