<a id="chromatin">Chromatin</a> - Is a method of organising and highly compacting [DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid). It is formed from a [complex](#complex) of various [molecules](#molecule) including [proteins](#protein), [RNA](#rna), and of course [DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid). Chromatin can be organised into two different forms; one which composes the other. [Euchromatin](#euchromatin) and [heterochromatin](#hetero-chromatin) respectively.
