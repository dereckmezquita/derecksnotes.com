<a id="apoptosis">Apoptosis</a> - The process of preprogrammed [cell](#cell) death which occurs in [multicellular](#multicellular) organisms. Do not confuse with [necrosis](#necrosis), which is caused by trauma. Apoptosis is a normal part of an organisms physiology.
