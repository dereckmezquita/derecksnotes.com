<a id="tetracycline-trans-activator-tta">Tetracycline Trans-activator (tTA)</a> - Is a fusion of a tetracycline-destabilising [DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid) binding domain and the [VP16](#vp16) [trans-activation domain](#tad). Thus, in the presence of [DOX](#doxycycline-dox) (an analog of tetracycline) tTA can no longer bind to its target [promoter](#promoter)
