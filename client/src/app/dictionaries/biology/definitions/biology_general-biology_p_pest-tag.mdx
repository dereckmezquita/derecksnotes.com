<a id="pest-tag">PEST Tag</a> - A [C-terminal](#c-terminal-region) tag that can be added to [proteins](#protein) to increase their [endogenous](#endogenous) [degradation](#degredation) rate. Termed "PEST" because the peptide is rich in proline (P), glutamate (E), serine (S), and threonine (T).
