<a id="cytomegalovirus-cmv">Cytomegalovirus (CMV)</a> - A [genus](#genus) of [viruses](#virus) in the [family](#family) of [Herepesviridae](#herpesviridae). Its promotor CMV is a strong viral [promoter](#promoter) commonly used in [mammalian](#z-dic-mammal) experiments and [synthetic biology](#synthetic-biology).
