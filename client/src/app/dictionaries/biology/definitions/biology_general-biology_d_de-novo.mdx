<a id="de-novo">*De Novo*</a> - From scratch. *exempli gratia:* *de novo* [DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid) synthesis is the synthesis of [DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid) from basic building blocks such as [nucleotides](#nucleotide) or [oligonucleotides](#oligonucleotide), instead of creating a new [DNA molecule](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid) that is a copy of an existing [DNA molecule](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid); that would be templated synthesis.
