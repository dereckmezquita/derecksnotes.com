<a id="complementary">Complementary</a> - Complementary is refering the physical matching of two corresponding parts, this may be seen simply as two puzzle pieces fitting together. This term is often used when refering to genetic material and more specifically a pair of strands composed of either [DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid)/[DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid) or [DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid)/[RNA](#rna) or [RNA](#rna)/[RNA](#rna), and even [proteins](#protein) with [DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid) or [RNA](#rna).
