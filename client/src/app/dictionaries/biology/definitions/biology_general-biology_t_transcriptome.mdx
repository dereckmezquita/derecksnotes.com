<a id="transcriptome">Transcriptome</a> - Refers to the set of all [RNA](#rna) molecules produced and contained by one [cell](#cell) or population of [cells](#cell).

 Transcriptomes can be studied and built by [DNA microarray assays](#dna-microarray) and [next-gen sequencing](#rna-seq) such as [RNA seq](#rna-seq).
