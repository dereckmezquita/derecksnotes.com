<a id="ribozyme">Ribozyme</a> - An [RNA molecule](#rna) that is capable of [catalysing](#catalysation) chemical reactions. Many ribozymes of interest (hammerhead and hairpin) [catalyse](#catalysation) [RNA](#rna) [cleavage](#cleavage) and [ligation](#ligation) reactions.
