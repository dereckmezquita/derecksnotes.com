<a id="3-prime-end-3">3 Prime End (3')</a> - The 3' or 3 prime end of a [DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid) or [RNA](#rna) molecule is the end that holds the hydroxyl group (\-OH). The other end of a nucleic strand is the [5' or 5 prime end ](#5-prime-end-5), which has the phosphate group (\-PO4). For an illustration see the figure for [DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid).
