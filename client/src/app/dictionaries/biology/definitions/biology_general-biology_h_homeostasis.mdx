<a id="homeostasis">Homeostasis</a> - The property of remaining stable in the face of changing external conditions. For example, maintaining a stable population of pancreatic [β-Cells](#β-cells) despite immune system malfunction resulting in Type I diabetes an artificial homeostasis system can be acheived by [synthetic biology](#synthetic-biology).
