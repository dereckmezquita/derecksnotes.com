<a id="marker">Marker</a> - In the context of [DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid) assembly, a [gene](#gene) that enables the screening or selection of [bacteria](#bacteria) containing our desired marker. This could be a [gene](#gene) that is toxic to the cell (negative selection; *exempli gratia* [ccdB](#ccdb)), a [gene](#gene) that confers antibiotic resistance (positive selection; *exempli gratia* [KanR](#kanamycin-resistance)), or a [gene](#gene) that turns the bacterial colonies some colour (*exempli gratia* [LacZ](#lacz), or a [fluorescent](#fluorescence) protein such as [RFP](#rfp)).
