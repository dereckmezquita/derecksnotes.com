<a id="mirna">miRNA</a> - A micro [RNA](#rna) is a functional [RNA](#rna) that can [inhibit](#inhibitor) the expression of a [gene](#gene) by either [inhibiting](#inhibitor) [translation](#translation) through mostly unknown mechanisms, or destabilising the [mRNA](#mrna) through the use of [enzymes](#enzyme) in the [RNA interference (RNAi)](#rna_interference_rnai) pathway. Not all organisms have the [RNAi](#rna_interference_rnai) pathway. Notable exclusions are all [prokaryotes](#prokaryote) and [baker's yeast, *S. cerevisiae*](#sachharomyces_cerevisiae).
