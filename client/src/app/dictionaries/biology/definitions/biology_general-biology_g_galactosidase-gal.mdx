<a id="galactosidase-gal">Galactosidase (GAL)</a> - Part of an [assay](#assay) frequently used in genetics, molecular biology, and other life sciences. An active [enzyme](#enzyme) may be detected using [X-Gal,](#x-gal) which forms an intense blue product after [cleavage](#cleavage) by [β-Galactosidase](#β-galactosidase-lactase-β-gal-beta-gal), and is easy to identify and quantify.
