<a id="galactosidase_gal">Galactosidase (GAL)</a> - Part of an [assay](#assay) frequently used in genetics, molecular biology, and other life sciences. An active [enzyme](#enzyme) may be detected using [X-Gal,](#x_gal) which forms an intense blue product after [cleavage](#cleavage) by [β-Galactosidase](#β_galactosidase_lactase_β_gal_beta_gal), and is easy to identify and quantify.
