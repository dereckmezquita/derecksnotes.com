<a id="heterochromatin">Heterochromatin</a> - [Euchromatin](#euchromatin) is wrapped into 30nm strcutures to form the megastructure known as heterochromatin. Heterochromatin is most often present in cells which are inactive, [senescent](#senescent), or otherwise dormant. This 30nm string composes the active [chromosome](#chromosome) during [interphase](#interphase).
