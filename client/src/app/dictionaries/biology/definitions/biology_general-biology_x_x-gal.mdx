<a id="x-gal">X-Gal</a> - A colourless [galactose](#galactose) [derivative](#derivative). When [cleaved](#cleavage) by [β-galactosidase](#β-galactosidase-lactase-β-gal-beta-gal) (encoded by the [LacZ gene](#lacz)), gives rise to two products: [galactose](#galactose) and an [indole](#indole). [Indole](#indole) spontaneously forms a [dimer](#dimer) which has a blue colour. This allows to test for the presence of [β-galactosidase](#β-galactosidase-lactase-β-gal-beta-gal) activity and by extension the activity or presence of the [LacZ gene](#lacz).
