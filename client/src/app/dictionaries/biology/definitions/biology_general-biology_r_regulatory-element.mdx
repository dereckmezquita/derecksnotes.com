<a id="regulatory-element">Regulatory Element</a> - Any element that regulates [gene expression](#gene-expression). Examples of [cis](#trans-cis)\-acting regulatory elements are operators, [promotors](#promoter), and leader peptides. [Trans](#trans-cis)\-acting regulatory elements are [diffusible molecules](#diffusion) such as [transcription factors](#transcription-factor-tf) or [trans](#trans-cis)\-acting [RNA](#rna) in a toehold switch system.
