<a id="vp16">VP16</a> - Herpes simplex virus (HSV) viral protein 16 (VP16) [trans-activation domain (TAD)](#tad). Stongly activates [gene expression](#geneExpression) in [eukaryotic](#eukaryota) [cells](#cell) when fused to a [DNA](#dna_desoxyribonucleic_acid) binding [protein](#protein), commonly Gal4.
