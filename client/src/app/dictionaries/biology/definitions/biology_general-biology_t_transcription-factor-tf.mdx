<a id="transcription-factor-tf">Transcription Factor, TF</a> - A [protein](#protein) that binds operator sequences in the [promoter](#promoter) or [enhancer](#enhancer) regions distal from the [promoter](#promoter). Activates or represses [transcription](#transcription) depending on the presence of a [trans-activation](#tad) or trans-repression domain, respectively. Also can contain a signal-sensing domain, which allows for external regulation of [DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid)\-binding or cellular localisation, *exempli gratia* by small [molecules](#molecule) or [hormones](#hormone).
