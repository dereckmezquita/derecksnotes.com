<a id="paratope_antigen_binding_site">Paratope (Antigen Binding Site)</a> - This is the part of an antibody which affixes itself to the [antigen](#antigen), it is composed of a small region of around 5 to 10 [amino acids](#amino_acid). For relevent information see [epitope](#epitope_antigenic_determinant).
