<a id="quantitative-polymerase-chain-reaction-qpcr">Quantitative Polymerase Chain Reaction (qPCR)</a> - [Quantitative](#quantitative) [PCR](#polymerase-chain-reaction-pcr). A method of determining the amount of a [DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid) sequence of interest that is present in a sample. By monitoring the [amplification](#amplification) of a strand of [DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid) during [PCR](#polymerase-chain-reaction-pcr), the original quantity can be [extrapolated](#extrapolation). qPCR can be used to monitor [gene expression](#gene-expression) by [reverse transcribing](#rtpcr) [mRNA](#rna) into [cDNA](#rtpcr) and then [quantifying](#quantitative) the [cDNA](#cdna) through qPCR.
