<a id="alphavirus">Alphavirus</a> - Small, spherical, [enveloped viruses](#enveloped-virus) with a genome of a single [positive (sense) RNA](#positive-rna-rna) strand. Ahphaviruses have been engineered into self replicating [RNA](#rna) and are commonly referred to as replicons.
