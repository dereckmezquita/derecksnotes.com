<a id="eukaryota">Eukaryota</a> - Organisms with membrane-bound [organelles](#organelle), and cytoskeletons. These organisms posses a [nucleus](#nucleus) which contains their genetic material, and is enclosed by the [nuclear envelope](#nucleus). Protists, plants, animals, and fungi, all fall into this domain of life.
