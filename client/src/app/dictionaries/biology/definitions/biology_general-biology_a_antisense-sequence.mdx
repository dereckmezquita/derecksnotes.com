<a id="antisense-sequence">Antisense Sequence</a> - A sequence of [nucleotides](#nucleotide) complementary to a [sense coding sequence](#sense-sequence), which may be either the strand of a [DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid) that undergoes [transcription](#transcription), or a [messenger RNA molecule](#mrna).
