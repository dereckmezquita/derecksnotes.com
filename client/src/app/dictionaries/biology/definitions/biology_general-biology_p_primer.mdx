<a id="primer">Primer</a> - A short, single-stranded [DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid) used in [DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid) replication. A primer binds a region of single-stranded [DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid) to form a primer-template complex which allows [DNA polymerase](#dna-polymerase) to synthesise the complementary strand (primer extension). Since most [DNA polymerases](#polymerase) are only able to catalyse the addition of a [nucleotide](#nucleotide) to the [3'](#3-prime-end-3) end of a [DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid) strand, primers are necessary for [elongation](#elongation). Primers are a necessary reagent for [PCR](#polymerase-chain-reaction-pcr).
