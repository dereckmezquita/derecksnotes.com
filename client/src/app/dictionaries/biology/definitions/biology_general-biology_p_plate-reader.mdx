<a id="plate-reader">Plate Reader</a> - A device for measuring the [absorbance](#absorbance), luminescence, or [fluorescence](#fluorescence) of bulk cell culture, *exempli gratia* suspended [bacteria](#bacteria). The name comes from reading the values of these properties in standard multi-well plate format, *exempli gratia* 96-well plates, where each well contains a sample.
