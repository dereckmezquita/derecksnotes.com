<a id="thermolabile">Thermolabile</a> - Refers to a [protein](#protein), [enzyme](#enzyme), or molecule that is (generally) negatively impacted or destroyed/[denatures](#denatures) by heat. An example would be a thermosensitive [origin of replication](#ori-r) or a thermolabile T5 [exonuclease](#exonuclease).
