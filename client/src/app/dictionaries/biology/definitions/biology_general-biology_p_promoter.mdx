<a id="promoter">Promoter</a> - A region of [DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid) that drives the [expression](#expression) of a [gene](#gene) following it on a piece of [DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid). Most [promoters](#promoter) are directional, meaning they drive the expression of a [gene](#gene) only on one side ([3'](#3-prime-end-3)) of it. Some [promoters](#promoter) are bidirectionally driven and will drive the expression on both the [5'](#5-prime-end-5) and [3'](#3-prime-end-3) side.
