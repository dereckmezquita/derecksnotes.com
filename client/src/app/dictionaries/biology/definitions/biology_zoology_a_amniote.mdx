<a id="amniote">Amniote</a> - Any of a group of land-dwelling vertebrates that have an amnion during embryonic development, including reptiles, birds, and mammals. Most extant mammals give live birth, the "egg" is retained inside the body during gestation; in humans the equivalent is known as amniotic sac.
