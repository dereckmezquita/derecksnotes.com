<a id="reversed_tetracycline_trans_activator_r_tta">Reversed Tetracycline Trans-Activator (r-tTA)</a> - Analogous to tTA, except that the function of [DOX](#doxycycline_dox) is reversed. [DOX](#doxycycline_dox) stabilises the [DNA](#dna_desoxyribonucleic_acid) binding domain, facilitating [promoter](#promoter) binding and activation of [gene expression](#geneExpression) of [promoters](#promoter) with tetracycline responsive elements (TREs). Referred to as the DOX-on system.
