<a id="reversed-tetracycline-trans-activator-r-tta">Reversed Tetracycline Trans-Activator (r-tTA)</a> - Analogous to tTA, except that the function of [DOX](#doxycycline-dox) is reversed. [DOX](#doxycycline-dox) stabilises the [DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid) binding domain, facilitating [promoter](#promoter) binding and activation of [gene expression](#gene-expression) of [promoters](#promoter) with tetracycline responsive elements (TREs). Referred to as the DOX-on system.
