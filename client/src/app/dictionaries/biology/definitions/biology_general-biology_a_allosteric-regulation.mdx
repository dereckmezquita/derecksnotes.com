<a id="allosteric-regulation">Allosteric Regulation</a> - Allosteric regulation happens when a [conformational change](#conformation) (the allosteric part) affects the function of a [protein](#protein) or [enzyme](#enzyme). For example, a small [molecule](#molecule) may bind a [transcription factor](#transcription-factor-tf) and cause a [conformational change](#conformation) that removes the [TF](#transcription-factor-tf)'s ability to bind [DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid), rendering it inactive.
