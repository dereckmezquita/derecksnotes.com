<a id="euchromatin">Euchromatin</a> - Euchromatin is a form of [chromatin](#chromatin) which is most often most present in active and [transcriptionally](#transcription) competent cells. [DNA](#dna_desoxyribonucleic_acid) is wrapped around [histone](#histone) proteins to form what are known as nucleosomes. This DNA/protein structure resembles beads on a string.
