<a id="multiple-cloning-site-mcs">Multiple Cloning Site (MCS)</a> - A designed stretch of [DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid) with recognition sites for many common [restriction enzymes](#restriction-enzyme). This region allows for the easy design, management, and [cloning](#cloning-molecular-cloning) of [DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid) into larger environments, such as [plasmids](#plasmid).
