<a id="upstream">Upstream</a> - A region of [DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid) is upstream of a sequence if it is on the [5'](#5-prime-end-5) side of the sequence of interest, it is always relative to some reference, *exempli gratia* the [promoter](#promoter) is upstream of the coding strand of a [gene](#gene).
