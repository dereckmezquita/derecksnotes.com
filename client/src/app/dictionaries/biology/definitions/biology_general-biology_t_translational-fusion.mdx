<a id="translational-fusion">Translational Fusion</a> - A [protein](#protein) fusion where the two [proteins](#protein) are [translated](#translation) together. The coding sequences of [translationally](#translation) fused [proteins](#protein) are on the same [mRNA](#mrna) and form a single [polypeptide](#polypeptide) chain when [translated](#translation).
