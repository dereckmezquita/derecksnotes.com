<a id="nucleus">Nucleus</a> - A [membrane-bound](#membrane-bound) compartment found in most [eukaryotic cells](#eukaryota). It typically contains most of the [cell](#cell)'s [genetic](#gene) information. This is the site of DNA replication and RNA synthesis. The entrance and exit of large [molecules](#molecule) to and from the nucleus is mediated by nuclear pore complexes in the nuclear membrane.
