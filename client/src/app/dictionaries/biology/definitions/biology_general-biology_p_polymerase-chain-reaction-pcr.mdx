<a id="polymerase-chain-reaction-pcr">Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR)</a> - A thermocycled reaction involving repeated rounds of primer annealing, primer extension and [dsDNA](#double-strand) denaturing to amplify and/or modify [DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid) sequences.
