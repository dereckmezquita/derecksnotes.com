<a id="ccdb">ccdB</a> - A lethal gene that targets [DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid) gyrase. The lethal gene is useful for ensuring that the [plasmid](#plasmid) containing it cannot be propagated in standard [*E. coli*](#escherichia-coli-e-coli) strains.
