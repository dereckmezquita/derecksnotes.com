<a id="bacterial-colony">Bacterial Colony</a> - A colony is a point of bacteria growing on a [Petri dish](#petri-dish). If the dishes are prepared correctly then this single point of bacteria should all be issue from the same stock, *i.e.* one single [cell](#cell) was deposited onto the medium and he is the one who [divided](#division) in order to produce the others in the colony. All cells in a colony should be genetically identical.
