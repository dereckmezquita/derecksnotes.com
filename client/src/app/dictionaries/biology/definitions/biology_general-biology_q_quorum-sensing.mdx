<a id="quorum-sensing">Quorum Sensing</a> - The ability of an organism to detect population density of others and or a [molecule](#molecule). The canonical quorum sensing system is the AHL-Lux system first described in *Aliivibrio fischeri* (formerly classified as *Vibrio fischeri*). Quorum sensing systems have been co-opted by [synthetic biologists](#synthetic-biology) to engineer cell-to-cell communication systems.
