<a id="transcription">Transcription</a> - [Transcription](#transcription) is the process of creating an [RNA](#rna) transcript based on information in [DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid). A [promoter](#promoter) drives [transcription](#transcription) and decides when and how much transcript is made. The transcript or resulting [mRNA](#mrna) is then transformed into a [protein](#protein) in a process called [translation](#translation).
