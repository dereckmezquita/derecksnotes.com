<a id="origin-of-replication-ori">Origin of Replication (ORI)</a> - The location in a genome (or within a replicating [plasmid](#plasmid)) at which [DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid) replication is initiated. [Genes](#gene) near origins of replication will typically have higher copy numbers on average.
