<a id="strain">Strain</a> - A particular variant of a [bacterium](#bacteria), [virus](#virus) or plant. For example, the [*E. coli*](#escherichia-coli-e-coli) strain BL21(DE3) has a particular [genotype](#genotype) setting it apart from other strains and making it useful for some applications and not others.
