<a id="operator">Operator</a> - The sequence of [DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid) to which a [transcription factor](#transcription-factor-tf) binds. Operators sequences can be varied to alter the binding affinity of their respective [transcription factor](#transcription-factor-tf).
