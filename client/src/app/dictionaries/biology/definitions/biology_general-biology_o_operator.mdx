<a id="operator">Operator</a> - The sequence of [DNA](#dna_desoxyribonucleic_acid) to which a [transcription factor](#transcription_factor_tf) binds. Operators sequences can be varied to alter the binding affinity of their respective [transcription factor](#transcription_factor_tf).
