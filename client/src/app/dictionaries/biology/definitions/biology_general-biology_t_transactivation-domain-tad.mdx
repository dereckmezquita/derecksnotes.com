<a id="transactivation-domain-tad">Transactivation Domain, TAD</a> - A domain of a [transcription factor](#transcription-factor-tf) that interacts with the [transcriptional](#transcription) machinery, cofactors, and/or [chromatin](#chromatin) modifying machinery (eukaryotes only) in order to facilitate formation of the [transcriptional initiation complex (TIC)](#tight-junction).
