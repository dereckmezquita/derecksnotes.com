<a id="strand">Strand</a> - A single [DNA molecule](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid) or [RNA](#rna). Such a strand has directionality, typically depicted as going from [5'](#5-prime-end-5) (the end of the strand with the [phosphate group](#phosphate-group)) to [3'](#3-prime-end-3) (the end of the strand with the -OH group).
