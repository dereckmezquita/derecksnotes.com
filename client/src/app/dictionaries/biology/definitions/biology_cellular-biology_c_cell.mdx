<a id="cell">Cell</a> - The fundamental structural unit of all life. The cell consists primarily of an outer [plasma membrane](#cell-membrane), which separates it from the environment; the genetic material ([DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid)), which encodes [heritable](#heredity) information for the maintainance of life; and the cytoplasm, a heterogeneous assemblage of ions, molecules, and fluid.
