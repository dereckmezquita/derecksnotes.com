<a id="nucleoside">Nucleoside</a> - A glycosylamine that is can be thought of as a [nucleotide](#nucleotide) without any [phosphate groups](#phosphateGroup). This term is commonly seen in the form of [desoxynucleoside triphosphates (dNTPs)](#desoxynucleosideTriphosphates), a necessary reagent for [PCR reactions](#polymerase_chain_reaction_pcr).
