<a id="nucleoside">Nucleoside</a> - A glycosylamine that is can be thought of as a [nucleotide](#nucleotide) without any [phosphate groups](#phosphate-group). This term is commonly seen in the form of [desoxynucleoside triphosphates (dNTPs)](#desoxynucleoside-triphosphates), a necessary reagent for [PCR reactions](#polymerase-chain-reaction-pcr).
