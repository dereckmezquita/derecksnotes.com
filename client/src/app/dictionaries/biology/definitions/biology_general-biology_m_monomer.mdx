<a id="monomer">Monomer</a> - The basic unit of a [polymer](#polymer); monomers compose up polymers. A [nucleotide](#nucleotide) is the [monomer](#monomer) of a strand of [DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid), which is a [polymer](#polymer) of [desoxyribonucleic acids](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid).
