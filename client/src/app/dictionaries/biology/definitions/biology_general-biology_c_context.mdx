<a id="context">Context</a> - The setting in which something occurs. Context is important in chemical or biochemistryical reactions, as some proteins or chemicals will not act or demonstrate normal activity relative to their chemical environments. This is often the case with proteins which will [denature](#denature) in the wrong pH.
