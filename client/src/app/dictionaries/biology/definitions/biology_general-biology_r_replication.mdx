<a id="replication">Replication</a> - The process of producing two identical copies of [DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid) from a single [double helix](#double-strand). [DNA](#dna-desoxyribonucleic-acid) replication begins at specific sequences termed [origins of replication](#origin-of-replication-ori) and requires [polymerases](#polymerase), [dNTPs](#desoxynucleoside-triphosphates), and other factors.
