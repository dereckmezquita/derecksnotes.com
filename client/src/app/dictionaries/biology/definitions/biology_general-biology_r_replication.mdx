<a id="replication">Replication</a> - The process of producing two identical copies of [DNA](#dna_desoxyribonucleic_acid) from a single [double helix](#doubleStrand). [DNA](#dna_desoxyribonucleic_acid) replication begins at specific sequences termed [origins of replication](#origin_of_replication_ori) and requires [polymerases](#polymerase), [dNTPs](#desoxynucleosideTriphosphates), and other factors.
