<a id="delta_h">ΔH (Enthalpy Change)</a> - Heat transferred at constant pressure. Key [thermodynamics](#thermodynamics) parameter.

Types:
- ΔH°f (formation)
- ΔH°c (combustion)
- ΔH°rxn (reaction)
- ΔH°soln (solution)

Sign convention:
- Negative: exothermic
- Positive: endothermic

Follows [hess_law](#hess_law) for multi-step processes.
