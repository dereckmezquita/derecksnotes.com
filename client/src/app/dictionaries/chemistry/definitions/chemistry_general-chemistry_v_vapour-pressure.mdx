<a id="vapour_pressure">Vapour Pressure</a> - Pressure exerted by vapour in [phase_equilibrium](#phase_equilibrium) with its liquid/solid. Related to [boiling_point](#boiling_point).

Clausius-Clapeyron equation:
$$
\ln\left(\frac{P_2}{P_1}\right) = -\frac{\Delta H_\text{vap}}{R}\left(\frac{1}{T_2} - \frac{1}{T_1}\right)
$$

Factors:
- Temperature
- [intermolecular_forces](#intermolecular_forces)
- Surface area
- External pressure

Application in [clausius_clapeyron](#clausius_clapeyron) analysis.
