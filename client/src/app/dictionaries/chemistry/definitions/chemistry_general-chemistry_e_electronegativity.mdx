<a id="electronegativity">Electronegativity</a> - An **atom's ability** to **attract electrons** in a [covalent_bond](#covalent-bond). Higher electronegativity differences between bonded atoms generally yield a larger [dipole_moment](#dipole-moment).

It differs from [electron_affinity](#electron-affinity) in scope: electronegativity applies to an atom in a bond, whereas electron affinity is an isolated atomic property. 
Pauling developed a numeric scale referencing bond energies and quantum insights (see [bohr_model](#bohr-model) evolution).
