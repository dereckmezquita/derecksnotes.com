<a id="2p-orbital">2p Orbital</a> - Set of three degenerate [orbital](#orbital)s with n=2, l=1. Essential in chemical bonding.

Characteristics:
- Three orientations (px, py, pz)
- Contains up to 6 electrons total
- One angular node each
- Dumbbell-shaped
- Important in [molecular_orbital](#molecular-orbital) formation

Key in [electron_configuration](#electron-configuration) and bonding.
