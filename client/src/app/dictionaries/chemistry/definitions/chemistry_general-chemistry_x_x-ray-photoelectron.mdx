<a id="x-ray-photoelectron">X-ray Photoelectron</a> - [spectroscopy](#spectroscopy) technique analysing surface composition via [photoelectric_effect](#photoelectric-effect).

Energy relation:
$$
E_K = h\nu - E_B - \phi
$$

Where:
- EK = kinetic energy
- h𝜈 = photon energy
- EB = [binding_energy](#binding-energy)
- φ = work function

Probes [core_electron](#core-electron)s:
- 1s: 0-1000 eV
- 2p: 50-200 eV
- 3d: 0-50 eV
