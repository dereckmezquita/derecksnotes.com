<a id="rate-determining-step">Rate-Determining Step</a> - Slowest [elementary_step](#elementary-step) in a [reaction_mechanism](#reaction-mechanism), controlling overall reaction rate.

Characteristics:
- Highest [activation_energy](#activation-energy)
- Determines [rate_law](#rate-law)
- Cannot be bypassed
- Focus for catalysis

Example mechanism:
$$
\begin{align*}
\text{A + B} &\rightleftharpoons \text{AB} \text{ (fast)} \\
\text{AB + C} &\rightarrow \text{ABC} \text{ (slow, RDS)} \\
\text{ABC} &\rightarrow \text{Products} \text{ (fast)}
\end{align*}
$$
