<a id="zeolite">Zeolite</a> - Microporous aluminosilicate with regular [crystal_structure](#crystal-structure). Acts as [molecular_sieve](#molecular-sieve).

Structure:
- SiO₄ and AlO₄ tetrahedra
- Uniform pore size
- Channel networks
- Exchangeable cations

Applications:
- [catalysis](#catalysis)
- [ion_exchange](#ion-exchange)
- Gas separation
- Water softening

Common types:
- ZSM-5
- Zeolite Y
- Mordenite
- Faujasite
