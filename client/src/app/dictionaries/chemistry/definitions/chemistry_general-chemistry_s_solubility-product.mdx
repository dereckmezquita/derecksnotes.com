<a id="solubility-product">Solubility Product</a> - [equilibrium_constant](#equilibrium-constant) Ksp for dissolution of sparingly soluble salt. Controls [precipitation](#precipitation).

For MₓAᵧ ⇌ xM⁺ + yA⁻:
$$
K_\text{sp} = [\text{M}^+]^x[\text{A}^-]^y
$$

Example values (25°C):
- AgCl: 1.8 × 10⁻¹⁰
- BaSO₄: 1.1 × 10⁻¹⁰
- Ca₃(PO₄)₂: 2.1 × 10⁻³³

Affected by [common_ion_effect](#common-ion-effect).
