<a id="valence-electron">Valence Electron</a> - Outermost shell electrons participating in [bonding](#bonding). Determines chemical behaviour and [oxidation_state](#oxidation-state)s.

Distribution in [periodic_table](#periodic-table):
- Group 1 (ns¹): 1e⁻
- Group 2 (ns²): 2e⁻
- Group 13-18: ns²np¹⁻⁶
- Transition metals: (n-1)d¹⁻¹⁰ns¹⁻²

Relation to [electron_configuration](#electron-configuration):
- s-block: ns electrons
- p-block: ns + np electrons
- d-block: (n-1)d + ns electrons
