<a id="oxidation">Oxidation</a> - Loss of electrons by a chemical species, resulting in increased [oxidation_state](#oxidation_state). Always paired with [reduction](#reduction).

Identification:
- Increase in oxidation number
- Loss of electrons
- Loss of hydrogen
- Gain of oxygen

Common oxidation half-reactions:
$$
\begin{align*}
\text{Fe}^{2+} &\rightarrow \text{Fe}^{3+} + \text{e}^- \\
\text{Zn} &\rightarrow \text{Zn}^{2+} + 2\text{e}^- \\
2\text{Cl}^- &\rightarrow \text{Cl}_2 + 2\text{e}^-
\end{align*}
$$
