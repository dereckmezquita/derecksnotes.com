<a id="x_ray_crystallography">X-ray Crystallography</a> - Technique determining [crystal_structure](#crystal_structure) using X-ray [diffraction](#diffraction) patterns.

[bragg_equation](#bragg_equation):
$$
n\lambda = 2d\sin\theta
$$

Key measurements:
- d-spacing
- Intensity
- Phase angles
- Miller indices

Determines:
- [unit_cell](#unit_cell) parameters
- Atomic positions
- Bond lengths/angles
- Crystal symmetry

Resolution limit ≈ λ/2.
