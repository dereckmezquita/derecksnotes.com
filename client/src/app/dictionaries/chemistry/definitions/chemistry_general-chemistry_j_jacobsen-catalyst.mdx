<a id="jacobsen-catalyst">Jacobsen Catalyst</a> - A **chiral manganese(III)** **Schiff base** complex developed by Eric Jacobsen. Used for **asymmetric epoxidation** of alkenes.

- Key features: **Salen-type ligand** forming a [coordination_chemistry](#coordination-chemistry) complex around Mn<sup>3+</sup>.  
- The catalyst's [oxidation_state](#oxidation-state) and electronic [delocalization](#delocalization) in the macrocyclic ligand enable selective oxidations.  
- Examples of usage: Synthesis of enantiomerically enriched epoxides, complementing other [catalyst](#catalyst) approaches.  

<Alert type='important'>
Jacobsen and Katsuki catalysts revolutionised green oxidation processes, offering high enantioselectivity without heavy-metal by-products.
</Alert>
