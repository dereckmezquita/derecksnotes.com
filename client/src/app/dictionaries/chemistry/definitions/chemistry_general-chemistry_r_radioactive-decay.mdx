<a id="radioactive-decay">Radioactive Decay</a> - Spontaneous nuclear transformation releasing particles or energy. Rate determined by [decay_constant](#decay-constant).

First-order kinetics:
$$
N(t) = N_0e^{-\lambda t}
$$

Types:
- α: He²⁺ emission
- β⁻: electron emission
- β⁺: positron emission
- γ: photon emission
- EC: electron capture

[half_life](#half-life) relation:
$$
t_{1/2} = \frac{\ln(2)}{\lambda}
$$
