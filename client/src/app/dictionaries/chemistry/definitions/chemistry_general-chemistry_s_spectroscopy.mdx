<a id="spectroscopy">Spectroscopy</a> - Study of matter through interaction with [electromagnetic_spectrum](#electromagnetic-spectrum). Based on [absorption](#absorption)/[emission](#emission).

Common types (wavelength):
- NMR: radio waves (>1m)
- IR: 2.5-25μm
- UV-Vis: 200-800nm
- X-ray: < 10nm

Energy transitions:
- NMR: nuclear spin
- IR: molecular vibration
- UV-Vis: electronic
- X-ray: core electrons

Relates to [quantum_levels](#quantum-levels).
