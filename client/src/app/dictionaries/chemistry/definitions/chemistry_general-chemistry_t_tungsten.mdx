<a id="tungsten">Tungsten</a> - [transition_metal](#transition_metal) (W, Z=74). Highest melting point of metals (3422°C). [refractory_metal](#refractory_metal).

Properties:
- Density: 19.25 g/cm³
- Common [oxidation_state](#oxidation_state)s: +6, +5, +4, +2
- Strong [metallic_bond](#metallic_bond)ing
- Resistant to corrosion

Key compounds:
- WO₃: yellow oxide
- WC: extremely hard carbide
- W(CO)₆: organometallic
- WS₂: lubricant
