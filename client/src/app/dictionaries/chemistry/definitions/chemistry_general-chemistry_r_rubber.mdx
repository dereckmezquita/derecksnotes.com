<a id="rubber">Rubber</a> - Natural or synthetic [elastomer](#elastomer) with high elastic deformation capacity. Strengthened by [vulcanisation](#vulcanisation).

Structure:
- cis-1,4-polyisoprene
- [polymer](#polymer) chains
- [cross_linking](#cross_linking) points
- Amorphous network

Properties:
- Glass transition: -72°C
- Elastic modulus: 0.1-0.5 MPa
- Maximum strain: 500-1000%
- Density: 0.92 g/cm³
