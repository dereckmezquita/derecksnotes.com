<a id="thermochemistry">Thermochemistry</a> - Study of [heat](#heat) changes in chemical reactions. Branch of [thermodynamics](#thermodynamics).

Key equations:
$$
\begin{align*}
q &= mc\Delta T \text{ (heat capacity)} \\
\Delta H &= \Delta E + \Delta(PV) \\
\Delta H_\text{rxn} &= \sum n\Delta H_\text{f} \text{ (products)} - \sum n\Delta H_\text{f} \text{ (reactants)}
\end{align*}
$$

Methods:
- [calorimetry](#calorimetry)
- Bomb calorimetry
- Solution calorimetry

Measures reaction [enthalpy](#enthalpy).
