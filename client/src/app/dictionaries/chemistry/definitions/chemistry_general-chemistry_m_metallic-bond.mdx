<a id="metallic_bond">Metallic Bond</a> - A **bonding model** for **metals** where **valence electrons** form a **'sea of electrons'** surrounding **positive metal ions**. This non-directional bonding grants metals distinct properties:

- **Malleability** and ductility ([malleability](#malleability)).
- High electrical [conductivity](#conductivity).
