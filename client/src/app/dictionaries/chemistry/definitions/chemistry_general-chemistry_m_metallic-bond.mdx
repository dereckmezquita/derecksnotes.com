<a id="metallic-bond">Metallic Bond</a> - A **bonding model** for **metals** where **valence electrons** form a **'sea of electrons'** surrounding **positive metal ions**. This non-directional bonding grants metals distinct properties:

- **Malleability** and ductility ([malleability](#malleability)).
- High electrical [conductivity](#conductivity).
- Partial correlation with [electron_affinity](#electron-affinity) and metal’s ability to release electrons into the communal pool.
- [enthalpy](#enthalpy) of vaporisation helps gauge metallic bond strength.

<Alert type='note'>
In metallic bonding, delocalised electrons move freely, explaining metals' lustre, thermal conduction, and mechanical behaviour.
</Alert>
