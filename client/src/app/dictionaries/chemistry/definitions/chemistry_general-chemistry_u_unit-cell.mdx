<a id="unit_cell">Unit Cell</a> - Smallest repeating unit of a [crystal_structure](#crystal_structure). Basic building block of [lattice](#lattice).

Common types (with [packing_efficiency](#packing_efficiency)):
- Simple cubic (52.4%)
- Body-centred cubic (68.0%)
- Face-centred cubic (74.0%)
- Hexagonal close-packed (74.0%)

Key parameters:
- Edge lengths (a, b, c)
- Angles (α, β, γ)
- [coordination_number](#coordination_number)
- Atoms per cell

Volume calculation:
$$
V = abc\sqrt{1 - \cos^2\alpha - \cos^2\beta - \cos^2\gamma + 2\cos\alpha\cos\beta\cos\gamma}
$$
