<a id="temperature">Temperature</a> - Measure of average molecular [kinetic_energy](#kinetic_energy). Determines direction of [heat](#heat) flow.

Scales and conversions:
$$
\begin{align*}
\text{K} &= \text{°C} + 273.15 \\
\text{°F} &= \frac{9}{5}\text{°C} + 32
\end{align*}
$$

Key points:
- Absolute zero: 0 K
- Triple point of water: 273.16 K
- Standard conditions: 298.15 K
- Controls [maxwell_boltzmann_distribution](#maxwell_boltzmann_distribution)

[thermal_equilibrium](#thermal_equilibrium) occurs at equal T.
