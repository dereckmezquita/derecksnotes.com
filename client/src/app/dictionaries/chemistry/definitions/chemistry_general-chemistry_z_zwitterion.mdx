<a id="zwitterion">Zwitterion</a> - Molecule with equal positive and negative charges. Common form of [amino_acid](#amino_acid)s at [isoelectric_point](#isoelectric_point).

Structure:
$$
\text{H}_3\text{N}^+-\text{R}-\text{COO}^-
$$

Properties:
- High [dipole](#dipole) moment
- pH dependent
- Highly water soluble
- [acid_base](#acid_base) buffer

pH effects:
- Low pH: cation
- pI: zwitterion
- High pH: anion
