<a id="viscosity">Viscosity</a> - Resistance to [flow](#flow) due to internal friction. Measured in Pa·s or poise (P).

Flow equation:
$$
\eta = \frac{F}{A}\frac{dx}{dv}
$$

Temperature dependence:
$$
\eta = \eta_0e^{E_a/RT}
$$

Common values (20°C, mPa·s):
- Water: 1.002
- Blood: 4.0
- Olive oil: 84
- Honey: ~10,000
