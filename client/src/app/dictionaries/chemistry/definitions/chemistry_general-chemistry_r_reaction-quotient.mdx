<a id="reaction_quotient">Reaction Quotient</a> - Ratio Q of product to reactant concentrations at any point. Compared with [equilibrium_constant](#equilibrium_constant) K.

For aA + bB ⇌ cC + dD:
$$
Q = \frac{[C]^c[D]^d}{[A]^a[B]^b}
$$

Relationships:
- Q < K: reaction proceeds forward
- Q = K: [chemical_equilibrium](#chemical_equilibrium)
- Q > K: reaction proceeds backward

ΔG relation:
$$
\Delta G = \Delta G° + RT\ln(Q)
$$
