<a id="stereochemistry">Stereochemistry</a> - Study of spatial arrangement of atoms in molecules and its effect on chemical properties.

Key concepts:
- [chirality](#chirality): non-superimposable mirror images
- [optical_activity](#optical-activity): plane polarised light rotation
- Conformations: rotations about single bonds
- Configuration: spatial arrangement

Types of [isomer](#isomer)s:
- Enantiomers (mirror images)
- Diastereomers (non-mirror stereoisomers)
- Conformers (rotational isomers)
- Geometric (cis/trans)
