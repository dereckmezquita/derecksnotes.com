<a id="colligative_properties">Colligative Properties</a> - **Solution properties** that depend on the **number of solute particles**, not their identity. Major colligative properties:

- **Boiling Point Elevation**: Solute addition raises the [boiling_point](#boiling_point).  
- **Freezing Point Depression**: Solute lowers the freezing point.  
- **Osmotic Pressure**: Pressure needed to stop solvent flow across a semipermeable membrane.  
- **Vapour Pressure Lowering**: Fewer solvent molecules at surface.

<Alert type='note'>
Techniques like [ebulioscopy](#ebulioscopy) rely on colligative properties. In real [solutions](#solutions), strong solute-solvent interactions can modify 'ideal' behaviour.
</Alert>
