<a id="gamma-radiation">γ-Radiation</a> - High-energy [electromagnetic_radiation](#electromagnetic-radiation) from [nuclear_transition](#nuclear-transition)s.

Properties:
- No mass or charge
- Highest penetration
- E = hν
- Wavelength < 10⁻¹¹ m

Nuclear equation:
$$
^A_Z\text{X}^* \rightarrow ^A_Z\text{X} + \gamma
$$

Represents [energy_level](#energy-level) transitions in nucleus.
