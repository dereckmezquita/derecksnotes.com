<a id="wavelength">Wavelength</a> - Distance between repeating units in wave. Key parameter in [electromagnetic_spectrum](#electromagnetic_spectrum).

Key relationships:
$$
\begin{align*}
c &= \lambda\nu \text{ (wave equation)} \\
E &= \frac{hc}{\lambda} \text{ (photon energy)} \\
n\lambda &= d\sin\theta \text{ (diffraction)}
\end{align*}
$$

Spectral regions:
- Î³-rays: < 10 pm
- X-rays: 10 pm - 10 nm
- UV: 10-400 nm
- Visible: 400-700 nm
- IR: 700 nm - 1 mm

Used in [spectroscopy](#spectroscopy) and [energy_level](#energy_level) analysis.
