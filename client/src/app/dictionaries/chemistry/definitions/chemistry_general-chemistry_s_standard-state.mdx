<a id="standard-state">Standard State</a> - Reference state for calculating [thermodynamic_properties](#thermodynamic-properties). Basis for ΔG°, ΔH°, ΔS°.

Conditions:
- Pure substance
- 1 bar [standard_pressure](#standard-pressure)
- Specified temperature (usually 298K)
- Unit [activity](#activity)

States:
- Gases: ideal at 1 bar
- Solutions: 1M
- Pure solids/liquids: normal state
- Elements: most stable form

Used in [gibbs_energy](#gibbs-energy) calculations.
