<a id="x-ray-diffraction">X-ray Diffraction</a> - Elastic scattering of X-rays by crystal [lattice](#lattice) planes. Reveals [crystal_structure](#crystal-structure).

Conditions:
- [wavelength](#wavelength) ≈ atomic spacing
- Regular crystal structure
- Coherent scattering
- Constructive interference

Analysis methods:
- Powder diffraction
- Single crystal
- Laue patterns
- Rotating crystal

Uses Cu Kα (λ = 1.5418 Å) or Mo Kα (λ = 0.7107 Å).
