<a id="infrared-spectroscopy">Infrared Spectroscopy</a> - An **analytical technique** that measures **molecular vibrations** or rotations, typically within the **infrared** region of the electromagnetic spectrum. 

- **Absorption Bands**: Molecules absorb at frequencies matching bond vibrational modes (stretching, bending).
- **Dipole Requirement**: IR absorption is strongest when a bond change alters the molecule's [dipole_moment](#dipole-moment).
- **Bond Insights**: Observing stretching in polar [covalent_bond](#covalent-bond) systems, and shifts from electron [delocalization](#delocalization).
- **Kinetics**: In some reaction studies, IR can track bond formation or cleavage, complementing [collision_theory](#collision-theory) ideas.
