<a id="psi-wavefunction">ψ (Wavefunction)</a> - Solution to [schrodinger_equation](#schrodinger-equation) describing quantum state. Foundation of [quantum_mechanics](#quantum-mechanics).

Properties:
- |ψ|² gives [probability_density](#probability-density)
- Must be continuous
- Must be single-valued
- Must be normalisable

Applications:
- [orbital](#orbital) shapes
- Energy levels
- Spectroscopy
- Bonding theory
