<a id="redox">Redox</a> - Coupled [reduction](#reduction) and [oxidation](#oxidation) reactions involving [electron_transfer](#electron_transfer). Changes in [oxidation_state](#oxidation_state).

Half-reactions:
$$
\begin{align*}
\text{Ox}_1 + ne^- &\rightarrow \text{Red}_1 \text{ (reduction)} \\
\text{Red}_2 &\rightarrow \text{Ox}_2 + ne^- \text{ (oxidation)}
\end{align*}
$$

Cell potential:
$$
E_\text{cell} = E_\text{cathode} - E_\text{anode}
$$

Common examples:
- Metal corrosion
- Battery discharge
- Photosynthesis
- Cellular respiration
