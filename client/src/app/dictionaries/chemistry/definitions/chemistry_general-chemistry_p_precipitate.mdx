<a id="precipitate">Precipitate</a> - Solid formed from solution during a chemical reaction. Formation governed by [solubility_product](#solubility_product) Ksp.

Formation condition:
$$
Q > K_\text{sp} \text{ where } Q = [\text{A}^+]^a[\text{B}^-]^b
$$

Key concepts:
- [ionic_equation](#ionic_equation) representation
- [crystal_growth](#crystal_growth) factors
- [supersaturation](#supersaturation) effects
- Temperature dependence

Common examples:
- AgCl: Ksp = 1.8 × 10⁻¹⁰
- BaSO₄: Ksp = 1.1 × 10⁻¹⁰
- PbI₂: Ksp = 7.9 × 10⁻⁹
