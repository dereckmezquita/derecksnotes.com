<a id="ozone">Ozone</a> - Triatomic oxygen (O₃) with bent structure. Strong [oxidising_agent](#oxidising-agent) with resonance stabilisation.

Structure:
- O-O bond length: 1.278 Å
- [bond_angle](#bond-angle): 116.8°
- Dipole moment: 0.53 D

Key reactions:
$$
\begin{align*}
\text{Formation: } &\text{O}_2 + \text{O} \rightarrow \text{O}_3 \\
\text{Decomposition: } &\text{O}_3 \rightarrow \text{O}_2 + \text{O} \\
\text{UV absorption: } &\text{O}_3 + h\nu \rightarrow \text{O}_2 + \text{O}
\end{align*}
$$

Resonance structures contribute to unusual stability and reactivity.
