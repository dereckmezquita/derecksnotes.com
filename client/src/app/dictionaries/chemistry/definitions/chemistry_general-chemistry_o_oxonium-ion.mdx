<a id="oxonium_ion">Oxonium Ion</a> - An oxonium ion is any oxygen cation with three bonds, the simplest form is H3O+ but in reality can be R3O+; R meaning any other groups. An oxonium ion is not a [hydronium ion](#Hydronium_Ion), this term refers specifically to ions which have hydrogens.
