<a id="salt">Salt</a> - [ionic_compound](#ionic_compound) formed from acid-base neutralisation. Contains cation (except H⁺) and anion (except OH⁻, O²⁻).

Properties:
- High melting points
- [crystal_lattice](#crystal_lattice) structure
- Variable [solubility](#solubility)
- [hydration](#hydration) tendency

Common examples (formula, solubility g/100mL):
- NaCl (36.0)
- KNO₃ (35.7)
- CaCO₃ (0.0013)
- AgCl (0.000195)
