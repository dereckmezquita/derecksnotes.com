<a id="quantum_theory">Quantum Theory</a> - Physical theory that energy exists in discrete units (quanta). Foundation of modern atomic theory.

Key equations:
$$
\begin{align*}
E &= hf \text{ (Planck)} \\
\lambda &= \frac{h}{mv} \text{ (de Broglie)} \\
\Delta x\Delta p &\geq \frac{h}{4\pi} \text{ (Heisenberg)}
\end{align*}
$$
