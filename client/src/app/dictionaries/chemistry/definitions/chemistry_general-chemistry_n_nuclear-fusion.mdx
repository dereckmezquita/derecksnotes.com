<a id="nuclear_fusion">Nuclear Fusion</a> - The combination of light atomic nuclei to form heavier elements, releasing substantial energy. Example:

$$
^2\text{H} + ^3\text{H} \rightarrow ^4\text{He} + n + 17.6\text{ MeV}
$$

- Requires extreme [temperature](#temperature) to overcome nuclear repulsion.
- Energy output explained by [mass_defect](#mass_defect).
- [binding_energy](#binding_energy) per nucleon peaks around iron.
- [nuclear_stability](#nuclear_stability) determines feasible fusion pathways.

<Alert type='note'>
Fusion powers stars and holds promise for future clean energy, though controlling plasma remains challenging.
</Alert>
