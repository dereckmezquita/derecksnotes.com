<a id="quaternary-structure">Quaternary Structure</a> - Spatial arrangement of multiple protein subunits in a functional protein complex. Builds on [tertiary_structure](#tertiary-structure).

Stabilising forces:
- [hydrogen_bond](#hydrogen-bond)s
- Hydrophobic interactions
- Salt bridges
- Van der Waals forces

Examples:
- Haemoglobin (4 subunits)
- Insulin (6 subunits)
- Antibodies (4 chains)

Influences [protein_folding](#protein-folding) and function.
