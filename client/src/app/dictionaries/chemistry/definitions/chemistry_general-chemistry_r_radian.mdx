<a id="radian">Radian</a> - SI unit of angular measure, defined as arc length equal to radius. Essential in [molecular_geometry](#molecular-geometry) and [vibration](#vibration) analysis.

Conversions:
- 2π rad = 360°
- π rad = 180°
- 1 rad ≈ 57.3°

Applications:
- [bond_angle](#bond-angle) measurement
- [orbital_hybridisation](#orbital-hybridisation) analysis
- Rotational spectroscopy
- Angular frequency (ω)
