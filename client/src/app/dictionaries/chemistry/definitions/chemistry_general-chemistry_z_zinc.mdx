- Ground state: [Ar]3d¹⁰4s²
- Common [oxidation_state](#oxidation-state): +2
- E°(Zn²⁺/Zn): -0.76V
- Amphoteric oxide ZnO

Key compounds:
- Zn²⁺: tetrahedral complexes
- ZnO: semiconductor
- Zn-finger proteins
- Brass (Cu-Zn alloy)
