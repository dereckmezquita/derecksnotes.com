<a id="zinc">Zinc</a> - [transition_metal](#transition_metal) (Zn, Z=30). Full d-shell [electron_configuration](#electron_configuration) gives unique properties.

Properties:
- Ground state: [Ar]3d¹⁰4s²
- Common [oxidation_state](#oxidation_state): +2
- E°(Zn²⁺/Zn): -0.76V
- Amphoteric oxide ZnO

Key compounds:
- Zn²⁺: tetrahedral complexes
- ZnO: semiconductor
- Zn-finger proteins
- Brass (Cu-Zn alloy)
