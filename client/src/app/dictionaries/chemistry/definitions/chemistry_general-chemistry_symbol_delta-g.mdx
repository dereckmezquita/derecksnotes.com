<a id="delta-g">ΔG (Gibbs Energy Change)</a> - Change in [gibbs_energy](#gibbs-energy) for a process. Determines [spontaneity](#spontaneity).

Key equations:
$$
\begin{align*}
\Delta G &= \Delta H - T\Delta S \\
\Delta G &= \Delta G° + RT\ln Q \\
\Delta G &= -RT\ln K_\text{eq}
\end{align*}
$$

Interpretation:
- ΔG < 0: spontaneous
- ΔG = 0: equilibrium
- ΔG > 0: non-spontaneous

Related to [enthalpy](#enthalpy) and [entropy](#entropy).
