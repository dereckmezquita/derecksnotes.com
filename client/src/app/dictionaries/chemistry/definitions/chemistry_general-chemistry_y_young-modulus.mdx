<a id="young_modulus">Young's Modulus</a> - Measure of material stiffness. Ratio of tensile [stress](#stress) to tensile [strain](#strain).

Definition:
$$
E = \frac{\text{stress}}{\text{strain}} = \frac{F/A}{\Delta L/L_0}
$$

Typical values (GPa):
- Diamond: 1220
- Steel: 200
- Aluminium: 69
- Rubber: 0.01-0.1
