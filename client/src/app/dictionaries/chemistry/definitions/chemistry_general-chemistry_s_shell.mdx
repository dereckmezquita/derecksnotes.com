<a id="shell">Shell</a> - Energy level occupied by electrons, designated by principal [quantum_numbers](#quantum-numbers) n (1,2,3...).

Capacity:
- n=1 (K): 2e⁻
- n=2 (L): 8e⁻
- n=3 (M): 18e⁻
- n=4 (N): 32e⁻

Subshells per n:
- n=1: 1s
- n=2: 2s, 2p
- n=3: 3s, 3p, 3d
- n=4: 4s, 4p, 4d, 4f

Follows [aufbau_principle](#aufbau-principle) filling order.
