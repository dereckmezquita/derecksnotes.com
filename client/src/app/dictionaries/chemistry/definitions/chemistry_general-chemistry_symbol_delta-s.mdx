<a id="delta_s">ΔS (Entropy Change)</a> - Change in molecular [disorder](#disorder). Key in [third_law](#third_law) thermodynamics.

Calculation:
$$
\Delta S_\text{surr} = -\frac{\Delta H_\text{sys}}{T}
$$

For processes:
- Phase changes
- Temperature changes
- Gas expansion
- Mixing/dissolution

Determines [spontaneity](#spontaneity) with ΔH.
