<a id="beta_particle">Beta Particle</a> - High-energy [electron](#electron) (β⁻) or [positron](#positron) (β⁺) emitted in [radioactive_decay](#radioactive_decay).

Decay equations:
$$
\begin{align*}
\text{β⁻: } &^A_Z\text{X} \rightarrow ^A_{Z+1}\text{Y} + \text{e}^- + \bar{\nu}_e \\
\text{β⁺: } &^A_Z\text{X} \rightarrow ^A_{Z-1}\text{Y} + \text{e}^+ + \nu_e
\end{align*}
$$

Properties:
- Continuous energy spectrum
- Greater penetration than α
- Less ionising than α
- Involves weak [nuclear_reaction](#nuclear_reaction)
