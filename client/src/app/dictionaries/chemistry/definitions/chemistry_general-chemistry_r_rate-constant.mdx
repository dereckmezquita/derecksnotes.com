<a id="rate-constant">Rate Constant</a> - Proportionality constant k in [rate_law](#rate-law) equations. Temperature dependence given by [arrhenius_equation](#arrhenius-equation).

Arrhenius equation:
$$
k = Ae^{-E_a/RT}
$$

Where:
- A = frequency factor
- Eₐ = [activation_energy](#activation-energy)
- R = gas constant
- T = temperature

Units depend on overall [reaction_rate](#reaction-rate) order:
- 0th order: mol·L⁻¹·s⁻¹
- 1st order: s⁻¹
- 2nd order: L·mol⁻¹·s⁻¹
