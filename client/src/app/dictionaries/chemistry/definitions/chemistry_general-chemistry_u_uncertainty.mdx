<a id="uncertainty">Uncertainty</a> - Range of doubt in a measurement. Combination of random and systematic errors affecting [precision](#precision) and [accuracy](#accuracy).

Calculations:
$$
\begin{align*}
\text{Absolute uncertainty} &= \pm \text{ value units} \\
\text{Relative uncertainty} &= \frac{\text{absolute uncertainty}}{\text{measured value}} \times 100\% \\
\text{Combined uncertainty} &= \sqrt{\sum(\text{uncertainty}_i)^2}
\end{align*}
$$

Rules for [significant_figures](#significant_figures):
- Addition/subtraction: least decimal places
- Multiplication/division: least sig figs
- Functions: least sig figs of input

Relates to quality of [measurement](#measurement).
