<a id="van-der-waals-forces">Van der Waals Forces</a> - Weak [intermolecular_forces](#intermolecular-forces) including [dipole](#dipole)-dipole and [london_dispersion](#london-dispersion) forces.

Energy relation:
$$
E = -\frac{C}{r^6}
$$

Strength factors:
- Molecular size
- [polarisability](#polarisability)
- Surface contact area
- Temperature

Typical energies:
- Dispersion: 0.1-1 kJ/mol
- Dipole-dipole: 1-10 kJ/mol
- H-bonding: 10-40 kJ/mol
