<a id="paramagnetism">Paramagnetism</a> - Property where substances are attracted to magnetic fields due to [unpaired_electron](#unpaired_electron)s. Strength determined by number of unpaired electrons.

Key aspects:
- [magnetic_moment](#magnetic_moment) μ = √(n(n+2)) BM
- Temperature dependent (Curie's Law)
- Common in transition metal compounds
- Explained by [crystal_field_theory](#crystal_field_theory)

Examples (unpaired e⁻):
- O₂: 2
- Cu²⁺: 1
- Fe³⁺: 5
- Cr³⁺: 3
