<a id="resonance">Resonance</a> - Description of delocalised electrons using multiple Lewis structures. Real structure is hybrid of resonance forms.

Key concepts:
- [electron_delocalisation](#electron-delocalisation)
- Equivalent resonance forms
- Fractional [bond_order](#bond-order)s
- Enhanced stability

Classic examples:
- Benzene: Kekulé structures
- Carbonate: three forms
- Ozone: two forms
- Nitrate: three forms
