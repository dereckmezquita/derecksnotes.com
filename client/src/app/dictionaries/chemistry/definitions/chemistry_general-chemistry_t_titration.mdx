<a id="titration">Titration</a> - Volumetric analysis determining unknown concentration via controlled reagent addition to [equivalence_point](#equivalence-point).

Types:
- [acid_base](#acid-base)
- Redox
- Complexometric
- Precipitation

Key points:
- Requires [standardisation](#standardisation)
- Uses chemical [indicator](#indicator)s
- Precise volume measurement
- Known stoichiometry

Common indicators (pH range):
- Methyl orange (3.1-4.4)
- Bromothymol blue (6.0-7.6)
- Phenolphthalein (8.2-10.0)
