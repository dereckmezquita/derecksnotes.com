<a id="1s_orbital">1s Orbital</a> - Spherically symmetric [atomic_orbital](#atomic_orbital) with principal [quantum_numbers](#quantum_numbers) n=1, l=0.

Properties:
- Lowest energy orbital
- Contains up to 2 electrons 
- No angular nodes
- Radial probability maximum at nucleus
- Radial function: R₁₀(r) = 2(Z/a₀)³/²e⁻ᶻʳ/ᵃ⁰
