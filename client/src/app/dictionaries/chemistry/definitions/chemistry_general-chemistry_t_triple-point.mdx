<a id="triple_point">Triple Point</a> - Temperature and pressure where solid, liquid, and gas phases coexist in [equilibrium](#equilibrium). Key feature of [phase_diagram](#phase_diagram)s.

Common values (T, P):
- H₂O: 273.16 K, 611.657 Pa
- CO₂: 216.55 K, 5.11 bar
- N₂: 63.18 K, 0.125 bar

Determines:
- Phase boundaries
- [vapour_pressure](#vapour_pressure)
- [phase_transition](#phase_transition) conditions
