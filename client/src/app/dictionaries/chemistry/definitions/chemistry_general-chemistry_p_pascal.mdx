<a id="pascal">Pascal</a> - SI unit of [pressure](#pressure) (Pa), defined as one newton per square metre (N/m²).

Common conversions:
- 1 atm = 101,325 Pa
- 1 bar = 100,000 Pa
- 1 torr ≈ 133.3 Pa
- 1 psi ≈ 6,895 Pa

Key reference points:
- [standard_pressure](#standard-pressure): 100 kPa
- [atmospheric_pressure](#atmospheric-pressure): 101.325 kPa
- Typical tyre pressure: 200-300 kPa
