<a id="voltaic-cell">Voltaic Cell</a> - Electrochemical cell converting chemical energy to electrical energy via spontaneous [redox](#redox) reactions.

Cell potential:
$$
E_\text{cell} = E_\text{cathode} - E_\text{anode}
$$

Components:
- Anode (oxidation)
- Cathode (reduction)
- [salt_bridge](#salt-bridge)
- External circuit

Standard reduction potentials E° (V):
- F₂/F⁻: +2.87
- Cl₂/Cl⁻: +1.36
- Cu²⁺/Cu: +0.34
- 2H⁺/H₂: 0.00
- Zn²⁺/Zn: -0.76

[electrode_potential](#electrode-potential) determines [electron_transfer](#electron-transfer).
