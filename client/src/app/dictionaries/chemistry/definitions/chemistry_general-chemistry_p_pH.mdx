<a id="pH">pH</a> - Negative logarithm of [hydronium_ion](#hydronium_ion) concentration. Measures acidity/basicity on 0-14 scale.

$$
\text{pH} = -\log[\text{H}_3\text{O}^+] = -\log[\text{H}^+]
$$

Key relationships:
- pOH = -log[OH⁻]
- pH + pOH = 14 (at 25°C)
- pH = ½[pKa - log(salt/acid)]
- [buffer_solution](#buffer_solution): pH ≈ pKa when [acid]=[base]

Common values (25°C):
- Strong acid: pH < 2
- Weak acid: pH 2-6
- Neutral: pH 7
- Weak base: pH 8-11
- Strong base: pH > 12
