<a id="ligand">Ligand</a> - An **ion** or **molecule** that **binds** to a **central metal** atom/ion via electron-pair donation, forming a coordination complex. Often acts as a [lewis_base](#lewis_base) with a lone pair.

- Can be monodentate (binds via one atom) or polydentate (multiple binding sites).
- Fundamental in [coordination_chemistry](#coordination_chemistry) and forming a [complex_ion](#complex_ion).
- Often involves a [dative_bond](#dative_bond) from ligand to metal.
- Examples: Water, ammonia, chloride, ethylenediamine, EDTA.
