<a id="nuclear_magnetic_resonance">Nuclear Magnetic Resonance (NMR)</a> - A [spectroscopy](#spectroscopy) technique analyzing atomic nuclei behavior in magnetic fields, crucial for structure determination.

- Measures [chemical_shift](#chemical_shift) of nuclei in different environments.
- Requires NMR-active [isotope](#isotope)s (e.g., ¹H, ¹³C, ¹⁹F, ³¹P).
- Different from electron spin [resonance](#resonance).
- Provides detailed molecular structure information.

<Alert type='note'>
NMR forms the basis of MRI medical imaging, though "magnetic resonance imaging" name avoids "nuclear" term.
</Alert>
