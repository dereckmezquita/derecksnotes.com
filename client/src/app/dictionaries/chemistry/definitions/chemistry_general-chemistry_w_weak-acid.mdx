<a id="weak_acid">Weak Acid</a> - Partial [ionisation](#ionisation) in water. Characterised by [acid_dissociation_constant](#acid_dissociation_constant) Ka.

Equilibrium:
$$
\text{HA} + \text{H}_2\text{O} \rightleftharpoons \text{H}_3\text{O}^+ + \text{A}^-
$$

Ka expression:
$$
K_a = \frac{[\text{H}_3\text{O}^+][\text{A}^-]}{[\text{HA}]}
$$

Common examples (Ka):
- CH₃COOH: 1.8 × 10⁻⁵
- HF: 6.8 × 10⁻⁴
- H₂CO₃: 4.3 × 10⁻⁷

Important in [buffer_solution](#buffer_solution) and [pH](#pH) calculations.
