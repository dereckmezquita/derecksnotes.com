<a id="oxidising_agent">Oxidising Agent</a> - Species that accepts electrons, causing oxidation of another species while being reduced itself. Strength measured by reduction potential (E°).

Strong oxidisers (E° > 0.9V):
- F₂ (2.87V)
- O₃ (2.07V)
- MnO₄⁻ (1.52V)
- Cl₂ (1.36V)
- Cr₂O₇²⁻ (1.33V)

Reaction pattern:
$$
\text{Ox} + n\text{e}^- \rightarrow \text{Red}
$$

<Alert type='important'>
Higher E° values indicate stronger oxidising agents.
</Alert>
