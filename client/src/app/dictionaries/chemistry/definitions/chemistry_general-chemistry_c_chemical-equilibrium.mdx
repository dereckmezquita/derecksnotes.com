<a id="chemical-equilibrium">Chemical Equilibrium</a> - A **dynamic state** where **forward** and **reverse** reaction rates match, so **concentrations** of reactants and products remain **constant**. Represented by an equilibrium constant $K_\mathrm{eq}$ for a [balanced_equation](#balanced-equation).

- **Rate Perspective**: [collision_theory](#collision-theory) ensures forward and reverse collisions occur at equal rates.  
- **Thermodynamics**: Equilibrium involves interplay of [enthalpy](#enthalpy) and [entropy](#entropy).  
- **Le Chatelier's Principle**: System shifts if conditions change (concentration, pressure, temperature).  

<Alert type='important'>
'Constant' concentrations do not mean the reaction stops; rather, forward and reverse processes continue at the same pace.
</Alert>
