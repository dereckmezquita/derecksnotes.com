<a id="joule">Joule</a> - The **SI unit** of **energy**, work, or heat, symbolised **J**. Defined as 1 newton-metre (N·m) or 1 coulomb-volt (C·V). In thermodynamics:

- Often used for [enthalpy](#enthalpy), internal energy changes, and measuring a system's [energy_level](#energy-level).
- [gas_constant](#gas-constant) in J·K<sup>-1</sup>·mol<sup>-1</sup> is a prime example of joule usage.
- Specific [heat_capacity](#heat-capacity) is typically expressed in J·K<sup>-1</sup>·g<sup>-1</sup> or J·K<sup>-1</sup>·mol<sup>-1</sup>.
