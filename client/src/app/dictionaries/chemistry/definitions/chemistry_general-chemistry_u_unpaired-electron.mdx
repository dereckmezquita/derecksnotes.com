<a id="unpaired-electron">Unpaired Electron</a> - Single electron occupying an [orbital](#orbital). Causes [paramagnetism](#paramagnetism) and influences reactivity.

Properties:
- [spin](#spin) = ±½
- Magnetic moment = √n(n+2) BM
- Contributes to multiplicity
- Affects bond formation

Common examples (number):
- O₂: 2
- NO: 1
- Fe³⁺: 5
- Cr³⁺: 3

Determined by [electron_configuration](#electron-configuration).
