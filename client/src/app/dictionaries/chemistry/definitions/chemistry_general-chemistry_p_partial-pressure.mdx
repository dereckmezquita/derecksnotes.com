<a id="partial_pressure">Partial Pressure</a> - The pressure exerted by one component of a gas mixture. Follows [daltons_law](#daltons_law): total pressure equals sum of partial pressures.

$$
P_\text{total} = \sum P_i = P_1 + P_2 + P_3 + ...
$$

Relationships:
- P₍ᵢ₎ = X₍ᵢ₎P_total where X = [mole_fraction](#mole_fraction)
- Influences [vapour_pressure](#vapour_pressure) equilibria
- Key in [gas_law](#gas_law) calculations
- Used in gas solubility (Henry's Law)
