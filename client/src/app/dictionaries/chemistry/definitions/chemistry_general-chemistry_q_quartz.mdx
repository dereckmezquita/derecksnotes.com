<a id="quartz">Quartz</a> - Crystalline silica (SiO₂), a [network_solid](#network_solid) with tetrahedral structure. Shows [polymorphism](#polymorphism).

Structure:
- Si-O-Si angle: 144°
- Si-O length: 1.61 Å
- [crystal_system](#crystal_system): trigonal
- Density: 2.65 g/cm³

Transitions:
- α → β at 573°C
- Tridymite: 867°C
- Cristobalite: 1470°C
- Melting: 1713°C
