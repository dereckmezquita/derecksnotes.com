<a id="3d-orbital">3d Orbital</a> - Set of five degenerate [orbital](#orbital)s with n=3, l=2. Critical in [transition_metal](#transition-metal) chemistry.

Shapes:
- dz²: dumbbell + ring
- dx²-y²: four-lobed planar
- dxy, dxz, dyz: four-lobed diagonal

Applications:
- [crystal_field_theory](#crystal-field-theory)
- Colour of complexes
- Magnetic properties
- [electron_configuration](#electron-configuration)
