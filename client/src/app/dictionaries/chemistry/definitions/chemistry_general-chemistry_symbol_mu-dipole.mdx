<a id="mu_dipole">μ (Dipole Moment)</a> - Vector measure of charge separation in molecules. Key in understanding [polarity](#polarity).

Calculation:
$$
\vec{\mu} = q\vec{d}
$$

Units:
- Debye (D)
- 1 D = 3.336 × 10⁻³⁰ C·m

Affected by:
- [electronegativity](#electronegativity) differences
- Molecular geometry
- Bond types
- [intermolecular_forces](#intermolecular_forces)
